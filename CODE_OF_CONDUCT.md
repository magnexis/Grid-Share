# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want GridShare to be a welcoming, respectful project for people interested in energy systems, software architecture, simulation, data science, and climate technology.

Contributors are expected to act with empathy, patience, and professionalism.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Give constructive feedback that focuses on the work.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Help keep discussions technically grounded and inclusive.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Dismissing safety, security, or reliability concerns without review.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.

If you need to report a concern, use the contact process in `SECURITY.md` for private issues or open a GitHub issue for non-sensitive community concerns.
